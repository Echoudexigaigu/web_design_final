FinBERT-Based Analysis of Fed Inflation Expectations

Feb 2025 – May 2025 · Supervisor: Prof. Adam Meyers (NYU)

  • Created a supervised dataset aligning 28,000+ paragraphs with quarterly CPI forecast changes.
  • Designed a sentence segmentation model to extract CPI-related content in FOMC minutes.
  • Replaced rSCHOLAR with FinBERT and significantly reduced processing time.
  • Planned evaluation via MAE, dispersion across policymakers, and correlation with market indicators.

Dataset

Aligned 28,000+ Greenbook paragraphs with quarter-over-quarter CPI forecast changes.

Model

FinBERT-based regression framework for continuous inflation expectation extraction.

Evaluation

MAE-based validation + dispersion analysis across policymakers + market correlation.
